Smash Burger Recipe

Allow Ground beef to come to temperature, heat skillet until it is screaming hot . Roll Beef into golf sized balls. Add thin layer of canola oil to pan until it shimmers. Add ground beef to skillet, and as soon as it hits the cook surface, squish meat down as flat as possible, after flattening continue flattening edges of burgers until all have tapered edges. After 3 minutes flip burger, add salt and cook three more minutes on opposite side. Pull off grill and place on bun

Ingrediants

  • Ground Beef
  • Cheese of your choice
  • Burger Bun
  • Condiments

Steps

  • Pre heat skillet, once hot add thin layer oil
  • Add round beef balls onto skillet and flatten, as well as flatten edges
  • After 3 minutes flip, add cheese
  • After 3 more minutes remove add to bun and serve
